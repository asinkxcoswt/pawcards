Little flashcards, made to share.

PawCards is a drawing-first flashcard app built for learning — and for having fun with friends. It's yours: it works offline, needs no account, and your cards live on your own device.

Your cards live on your device

Everything you make is stored right on your phone — not on our servers. There's no sign-up, no email, no account, and we don't track you or show ads. Open the app on an airplane and it just works.

Good to know

You never have to connect anything. If you use PawCards on one device and don't need to share, you can stay completely local — forever. Your cards are yours, and you can export a backup file anytime.

When you might want to connect a server

PawCards can optionally sync through a server — but only when you want one of these:

  • →More than one device. Keep your phone and tablet in sync.
  • →A safety net. Back up your cards so a lost phone doesn't mean lost decks.
  • →Sharing with friends. Send a deck, or run a room/workshop where everyone reviews together.
  • →AI images. The ✨ button that draws your card fronts runs on a small server.

And here's the important part: the server is yours, not ours. Sync runs through your own Cloudflare worker (or one a friend shares for a workshop). It's a courier that moves your cards between your devices — it isn't an account, and we never see your content. No server, no problem: the app is fully usable without one.

Made for friends 🐾

Learning sticks better together. Share a deck with a tap, join a room to swap decks with a study group, or run a live group review where everyone follows along. When you invite people, you're in control of exactly what you share — down to whether they can use your server at all.

Questions

Is it free?

The app is free and works fully offline with no account. AI image generation runs on a server (yours or a shared one), which has daily limits — that's the only piece with a real-world cost.

Do I need an account?

No. There are no accounts at all. If you sync across devices, your identity is a private "Sync ID" you copy between them — like a key you keep, not a login you register.

Where is my data?

On your device, in the app's local storage. If you turn on sync, a copy also lives on the server you chose (your own Cloudflare worker). We never store or see your cards.

Can I use it without the internet?

Yes — creating cards, drawing, and reviewing all work offline. You only need a connection to generate AI images or to sync/share.

What if I lose my phone?

If you never synced, cards live only on that device — so export a backup now and then. If you did sync, just enter the same Sync ID on a new device and pull them back.

I'm technical — can I run my own?

Yes. PawCards is designed for you to bring your own Cloudflare worker, so you host everything yourself and depend on no one. See the setup guide from the app.
